SchoConnect Legal Policies

Privacy & Data Protection

SchoConnect does not require user accounts for mentees and does not collect any personal data from students. All payments are processed securely via Razorpay; we do not store any banking or card details. Mentors voluntarily provide their professional or educational background; this information is not shared without explicit consent. SchoConnect values user privacy and adheres to all applicable data protection standards.

Platform Role & Disclaimer

SchoConnect is a facilitation platform that connects students with mentors. All guidance provided by mentors is based on their personal knowledge and experiences and should not be interpreted as professional, legal, financial, or medical advice. We do not guarantee any specific results or outcomes from mentorship sessions. Students and users are advised to exercise discretion and critical thinking when acting on any guidance received through the platform.

Payments, Cancellations & Liability

All transactions are securely handled by Razorpay. Any payment disputes must be directed to Razorpay. In the case of session cancellations, either a refund or rescheduling will be offered based on mutual agreement between the student and mentor. SchoConnect shall not be held liable for: Any advice or suggestions offered by mentors. Any personal outcomes or consequences of mentorship sessions. Any communication or interaction between mentors and students.
